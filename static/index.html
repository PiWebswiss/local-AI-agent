Voice input

        
When enabled, a Talk button appears in the chat composer. Clicking it records from your microphone and sends the clip to the local WhisperX service for transcription. Audio never leaves your machine.

        
        

      


      

        
Context compression

        
Long sessions get summarized so the model keeps a compact briefing of earlier turns instead of the raw transcript.

        
        

          
          
        

      


      

        
Sub-agent spawning

        
When the planner detects a multi-part request, it splits it into independent sub-tasks (chat + research), runs them in parallel, then synthesizes one answer.

Enable voice input?

      
This local agent can transcribe your voice with WhisperX so you can talk to the model instead of typing. Microphone access happens only when you click the Talk button — nothing is recorded otherwise, and audio never leaves your machine.

      
You can change this later in Settings → Voice input.
